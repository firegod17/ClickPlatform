Please pay attention to these rules! In order to pass verification please download the following documents:

Mandatory requirements for documents

  • Scanned copies or photos should be made from the original, in good quality, all the details of the documents are clearly displayed.

  • Color scanned copies or photographs made on a plain background.

  • Displayed in full; cutting the edges of the document is not allowed, all 4 (four) corners of the document must be visible

  • It should not contain foreign objects, objects or inscriptions that are not on the original document (for example, paper clips, pencils, fingers, etc.)

  • Edited in a graphic editor OR containing scanning / photographing artifacts (flash trace, blur) - are not accepted

  • Files are accepted in jpg or png formats ranging in size from 500kb to 5Mb

To confirm your address you can download one of the following documents:

  • Statement from the bank

  • Utility bill

  • Tax documents

  • A document confirming your registration and issued
